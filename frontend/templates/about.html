💼 About Our System

The Secure Healthcare Crypto System is a comprehensive platform designed to meet the demanding security requirements of modern healthcare organizations. Built with cutting-edge cryptographic technologies, our system ensures that sensitive patient data remains protected at all times.

Our platform integrates hybrid encryption (combining the efficiency of AES-256 with the security of RSA-2048), role-based access control (RBAC), digital signatures, and tamper-evident audit logging to provide a complete security solution for healthcare providers.

🚀 Key Features

🔒

Hybrid Encryption

Advanced hybrid cryptography combining AES-256-GCM for speed and RSA-2048 for secure key exchange. Ensures your data is protected with industry-leading encryption standards.

👥

Role-Based Access Control

Granular access control with multiple user roles (Doctor, Nurse, Admin, Patient). Each role has specific permissions ensuring data is only accessible to authorized personnel.

✍️

Digital Signatures

All communications are digitally signed ensuring authenticity and non-repudiation. Verify the source and integrity of every message and document.

📊

Audit Logging

Comprehensive, tamper-evident audit logs with cryptographic hash chaining. Every action is recorded and can be verified for compliance and forensic purposes.

📧

Secure Email

End-to-end encrypted email communication for healthcare data. Send and receive sensitive information safely with attachments and digital signatures.

⚔️

Tamper Detection

Advanced tamper detection systems that identify any unauthorized modifications to payloads or audit logs. Red team testing capabilities for security validation.
