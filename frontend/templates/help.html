FAQ

How secure is my data?

We use AES-256 in GCM mode, providing both encryption and authenticity. Keys are never stored in plaintext and are protected using RSA-2048.

What is the "Cipher Image"?

It is an encrypted binary version of a medical image. It looks like random data without the correct RSA private key.

Can I reset my password?
